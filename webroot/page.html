How to Play

Reel Riddle is a movie guessing game where you try to identify movies based on mysterious descriptions.

  • Read the AI-generated description of a movie
  • Type your guess in the input field
  • Hit the "Guess" button to check your answer
  • Need help? Click the "Hint" button for the first letter of each word

You can also create your own riddles for others to solve by entering your favorite movies!
